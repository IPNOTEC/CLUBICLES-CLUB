HOW IT WORKS

1. Join as a Member

Invest ₹1 Lakh to become a member. You instantly get a 30% discount on all bookings and become part of the 7% ownership pool of CLUBICLES LLP LTD.

2. List as a Space Owner

If you own a space, you can use our platform for bookings and data management. If you are also a paid member, your space gets 24-hour activation and 24/7 support.

3. Explore as a Scout

Join our Scout Team to find and verify new locations for co-working spaces, helping us grow our global network from the ground up.

4. Membership Withdrawal

  • Your ₹1 Lakh membership fee can be withdrawn at any time.
  • Upon withdrawal, you lose access to all membership features (discounts, ownership, etc.).
  • If withdrawn within the first 12 months, a ₹10,000 processing fee applies.
